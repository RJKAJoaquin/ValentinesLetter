How would spreading the word help?

Spreading the word can help increase awareness of the problem at hand, that being climate change, and increases the amount of people willing to fight against climate change because, believe it or not, we can't stop climate change alone.


Where do I start?

You can start small and spread awareness within your family, friends, and other people close to you. For a bigger potential audience, you can start spreading your message through social medias like facebook, instagram, and twitter. At the end, it shouldn't matter as long as you spread the word and make sure you let everyone know to spread the word as well
