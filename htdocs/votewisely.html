How can voting help?

Voting isn't just about politics as some may suggest. It is about letting your thoughts and opinions shape the way the government runs, be it local or national. As citizens, it is one of the only tools we have to guide the government in the right direction. If you're unsure about which of your representatives would likely take action against climate change, it's best to do research on their prior decisions, or even contact them yourself if possible
