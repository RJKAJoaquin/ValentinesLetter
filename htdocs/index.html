On this valentines day, I dedicate this sonnet to you, my mother. Love, Raffy <3
That I was born with a sun such as thou
In truth, the luckiest of all was me
Although all have life, all have energy
You give me so much more, I will avow
You shine so bright all day, I wonder how
The endless warmth of yours envelops me
You’re always warm for me, Impossibly
But when dusk comes, with all my heart, I vow
That I will shine my very best for you
That on your heart, I’ll place all warmth upon
That I will shine to keep the dark from you
Like how, for me, you kept the brightness on
And when the pitch black night is true
I’ll take it in pride that I was your son
